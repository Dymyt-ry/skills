## Production Notes

- All scripts written for vertical video (9:16), phone-to-face or screen-share style
- Each CTA ends with: "Try it free at spndr.dev"
- Script 3 works best with a reaction cut when the "roast" lines land — pause after each burn
- Script 1 works with B-roll of phone + app UI
- Script 2 works with split screen: bank app on one side, spndr on the other
- Recommended music: lo-fi or ambient (non-distracting) for Scripts 1 and 2; trending sound for Script 3
