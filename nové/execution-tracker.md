### 🔥 PRIORITY: HN "Show HN: Trackm" thread (22 comments, posted 3 days ago)
**URL:** https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47406569
**Reply to:** deweller's comment ("Plaid and the like are too expensive...")

> Plaid is actually more accessible than people think if you go through their startup/growth program. We got access for spndr (spndr.dev) without the full enterprise pricing — it took a few back-and-forths with their team but they're fairly reasonable for small teams.
>
> The other thing worth noting: the bank integration problem is nearly solved infrastructure at this point. The hard part is what you do with the data after. We built spndr at a hackathon in Slovakia last month specifically because we wanted proactive intelligence, not just "here's your spending history." The AI layer that sends you alerts before you overspend, or flags tax deductions you're missing — that's where the moat actually lives.
>
> Forward projection (what you built) is a very different angle and genuinely underexplored. Most people don't need to know what they *will* spend — they need to know what they *should* do right now. You've made a smart bet on the planning vs. tracking distinction.
