## Description (300 words)

Most personal finance apps show you what happened. spndr helps you do something about it.

**The problem:** If you have a salary, a side hustle, multiple bank accounts, and investments scattered across five apps — you have no real picture of your money. Your bank categorizes transactions. It doesn't optimize them. Your accountant sees your finances once a year. spndr sees them in real time.

**What spndr does:**
Connect your accounts in under 30 seconds (5,000+ banks via Plaid). From there, an AI agent automatically categorizes every transaction, audits your subscriptions for waste, surfaces tax deductions you're missing, and gives you a complete financial picture across all your accounts — not just the one you opened this app with.

Our users find an average of **$2,300 in missed tax deductions** per year. That's not a marketing number — it's what happens when you stop letting transactions be transactions and start treating them as data.

**Who it's for:** Freelancers, consultants, creators, and anyone with more than one income stream. If you're earning more than you have time to track, spndr was built for you.

**Why spndr vs. Mint/YNAB/Cleo?**
- Mint is dead. YNAB requires manual input and a PhD in budgeting. Cleo is a chatbot.
- spndr is a proactive AI agent — it works while you sleep and alerts you when something matters.
- Free tier, forever. No ads. No selling your data.

**What's included:**
- Free: unlimited account connections, AI categorization, subscription audit
- Pro ($9/mo): tax optimization, multi-income reports, export-ready tax docs
- Max ($29/mo): full AI tax analysis, deduction tracking, accountant-grade reports

We're launching today. Early users get 2 months Pro free if they upgrade this week.
