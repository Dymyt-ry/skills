## What spndr Does

spndr is an AI Finance Agent that connects to your bank accounts (read-only), categorizes spending automatically, detects subscriptions, spots anomalies, and delivers proactive financial insights via Telegram.

### How It Works

| Step | What happens |
|------|-------------|
| **01 — Connect your banks** | Link all accounts in 2 minutes. Read-only access. |
| **02 — AI does the work** | Categorizes spending, detects subscriptions, spots anomalies. Automatically. |
| **03 — spndr comes to you** | Morning briefings, spending alerts, budget coaching. Delivered to Telegram. |

### Key Features

- Cross-bank aggregation (all accounts in one view)
- AI-powered chat (ask questions about your spending)
- Proactive alerts & morning briefings
- Subscription detection
- Spending personality modes
- "Can I afford this?" real-time analysis
- Multi-income support (freelancers, gig workers, side hustles)
- No app download needed (lives in Telegram)

## Brand Strategy

### Brand Purpose

We exist to give busy professionals financial clarity without the work. No spreadsheets, no manual tracking, no anxiety.

### Brand Values

1. **Clarity** — We make the complex simple. One dashboard, one view, zero confusion.
2. **Proactivity** — We come to you before problems happen, not after.
3. **Honesty** — We show the real picture, even when it's uncomfortable.
4. **Simplicity** — If it takes more than 2 minutes, we've failed.
5. **Autonomy** — Your money, your data, your control. Always.

### Brand Personality

If spndr were a person: a sharp, confident financial advisor who texts you before you ask. Not stuffy or corporate — more like a smart friend who happens to be great with money.

| Attribute | What it means | What it's NOT |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| **Confident** | We know our stuff and show it with data | Arrogant or preachy |
| **Direct** | We say what matters, skip the fluff | Rude or dismissive |
| **Witty** | We use humor to make finance approachable | Trying too hard, meme-heavy |
| **Modern** | Clean, minimal, tech-forward | Cold or impersonal |

## Tone of Voice

### Spectrum

| Dimension | Position (1-5) | Description |
|-----------|:--------------:|-------------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | **4** | Casual but not sloppy. Like texting a smart friend. |
| Serious ↔ Playful | **3.5** | Mostly serious about money, but with wit. |
| Respectful ↔ Irreverent | **3** | Respectful of the user's situation, but willing to call out bad habits. |
| Enthusiastic ↔ Matter-of-fact | **3** | Data-driven, not hype-driven. Let numbers speak. |
| Technical ↔ Simple | **4.5** | Always simple. Finance is already scary enough. |

### Writing Rules

**We say:** customer, help, money, track, save, simple, clear, connect
**We avoid:** leverage, synergy, disruptive, revolutionary, utilize, facilitate

**Contractions:** Always (we're, you're, it's, don't)
**Active voice:** Always ("We help you" not "You are helped by us")
**Numbers:** Use numerals for impact ($2,354 not "two thousand")

### On-Brand vs Off-Brand Examples

| Context | Off-Brand | On-Brand |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Feature launch | "We are pleased to announce our revolutionary AI-powered financial optimization module." | "New: spndr now catches forgotten subscriptions before they renew." |
| Error message | "Error 500: Internal server error. Please try again later." | "Something went wrong on our end. We're on it. Try again in a minute." |
| CTA | "Register now to begin your financial journey." | "Connect your bank. Takes 30 seconds." |

## Social Media Post Rules

Every spndr social media slide MUST include:

1. **`spndr.` watermark** — bottom-left, small, muted gray text with orange dot
2. **Slide counter** — top-right corner (e.g. `1 / 3`), small muted text

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use exact hex codes (`#0a0a0a`, `#f97316`)
- Keep it minimal — one idea per slide/section
- Let data speak (numbers > adjectives)
- Use `spndr.` watermark on every visual asset
- Address the user directly ("you", "your")
- Dark backgrounds for social, cream for pitch deck

### Don't
- Use "revolutionary" or "disruptive"
- Add custom fonts (system only)
- Use colors outside the palette
- Make slides text-heavy (max 30-40 words per slide)
- Use stock photos that look staged
- Forget the orange dot in `spndr.`
