## Logo Usage

- On dark backgrounds: white logo (`spndr-logo-white-transparent.png` or `.svg`)
- On light backgrounds: black logo (`spndr-logo-black-transparent.png` or `.svg`)
- Social avatar: `spndr-social-avatar-dark.png` (preferred for dark-mode platforms)
- The dot in "spndr." is always orange `#f97316`

## Photo Style

- Professional, aspirational lifestyle photography
- High-income context: modern architecture, premium workspaces, coffee shops
- Diverse representation
- Dark gradient overlay from bottom to integrate with text
- Warm golden/amber tones complement the orange accent

## Tone of Voice

- Confident but not pushy
- Clean, direct sentences
- Problem-aware: acknowledge the pain of financial complexity
- Solution-forward: spndr makes it effortless
- Never use jargon or financial buzzwords
- Speak to busy high-earners who value time
