## Hook Formulas

Adapt any hook using these patterns:

1. **Contrarian**: "Everyone says [common advice]. Here's why that's wrong."
2. **Stat shock**: "[Surprising number] — and most people have no idea."
3. **Question**: "Do you know [thing they probably don't know]?"
4. **Before/After**: "[Bad state] → [Good state]. Here's the bridge."
5. **Scarcity**: "This [tip/feature] saves the average person $X/month."
6. **Identity**: "If you earn $X+, you need to see this."
